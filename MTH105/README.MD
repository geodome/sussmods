## Sypnosis 

MTH105 Fundamentals of Mathematics will introduce students to the language, notions and methods upon which a sound education in mathematics at the university level is built. 

Students will be exposed to the language of mathematical logic, the idea of rigorous mathematical proofs and fundamental mathematical concepts such as sets, relations and functions.  

## Topics

* Predicates
* Quantified Statements
* Direct Proof
* Counterexamples
* Contradiction and Contraposition
* Algorithms
* Sets
* Set Theoretic Operations
* Mathematical Induction
* Relations
* Equivalence Relations
* Functions
