## MTH107 Calculus I

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

MTH107 Calculus I will introduce students to the study of limits, continuity and differential calculus.  

Students will be exposed to computational techniques in evaluating limits and derivatives and applications of differentiation to optimization.  

The course will be foundational to everyone who wants to delve further into optimization.
