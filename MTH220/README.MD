## MTH220 Statistical Methods and Inference

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

MTH220 is a natural continuation of MTH219 and aims to equip students with more in-depth statistical knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems and to make intelligent decisions.  

Students will be able to apply a wide range of concepts and statistical methods for analysing data, carrying out hypothesis testing, estimation and drawing inferences.  

Correlation analysis, regression methods, non-parametric techniques and inferential statistics will be introduced.
