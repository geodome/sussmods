## ENG301 Microprocessor Programming 

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

ENG301, Microprocessor Programming is intended to introduce the programming fundamentals of the ARMv7 microcontroller system, its instruction set, and the 
programming/control of ARMv7 based systems.  

To enhance the understanding and application of ARMv7 microcontroller, the  classical ARM architecture and assembly language programming, ARM organization and implementation and architecture support for system development are comprehensively discussed.
