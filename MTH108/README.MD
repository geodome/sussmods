## MTH108 Calculus II

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

MTH108 will be a continuation to MTH107 and introduce students to integral calculus.  

Students will be exposed to computational techniques such as the various techniques to integration as well evaluating the derivatives of integrals and inverse functions and applications of integration such as areas between curves and volumes of cylindrical shells.
