## MTH320 Mathematical Methods II

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

MTH320 Mathematical Methods II gives a comprehensive introduction to more advanced topics such as solving ordinary differential equations with power series solutions, using Laplace transforms to solve ordinary differential equations and solving partial differential equations via Fourier series.  

Relevant physical models such as vibrating string, heat flow from a body in space and membranes will be taught alongside with the mathematics.
