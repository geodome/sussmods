## MTH219 Introduction to Probability and Statistics

These past year paper solutions cater to Mathematics and 
Programming Academic Units at SUSS.  

If you need tuition, feel free to contact me at maths_and_programming@outlook.sg.  

Students are exposed to the essential and important concepts of Statistics and Probability for data analysis.  

Illustrative examples in various discipline will be discussed.  

Emphasis will be on understanding data variability and uncertainty; cultivating statistical thinking and applying statistical techniques to solve real-life prctical problems.  

Descriptive statistics and useful probability models will be introduced.
